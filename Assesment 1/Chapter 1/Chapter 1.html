Hamburger Helper Recipe

This is a simple delicious homemade version of the classic hamburger helper!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1 small onion diced
  • 2 cups of elbow macoroni
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • add salt and pepper for your desired taste

Method

  1. In a large skillet, you first cook the ground beef and the diced onion over a medium heat until the beef is brown and drain the excess fat.
  2. Then you add the beef broth, milk, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and the pepper while stirring it.
  3. You now stir in the elbow macaroni into the skillet and bring it into a simmer.
  4. Cover and cook it for abot 10 minutes but stir it occasionally, until the pasta is tender.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and add in the shredded cheese and stir until melted and creamy.
  6. Put into a plate and enjoy!
