Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game that was developed by Mojang studios. This game allows players to explore, build, and survive in a blocky world.

Key Information

Developer: Mojang Studios

Release Date: November 18, 2011

Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, Nintendo Switch, Mobile

Genre: Sandobx, Survival

Characters

The game has recognizable characters such as Steve and Alex.

Reviews

Minecraft is one of the best-selling games of all time, it is highly praised for its open-world mechanics and creativity.
